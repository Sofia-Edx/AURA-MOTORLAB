Collision Repair

When the unexpected happens, trust your vehicle to technicians who understand what it means to drive something extraordinary. We restore European and exotic vehicles to factory specification — or better.

Service 01

Collision Repair & Body Shop

At Aura Motor Lab, our collision repair facility is purpose-built for high-value European and exotic vehicles. Every panel, every seam, every structural component is addressed with the same precision engineering your car received at the factory.

We work directly with insurance companies and accept all major carriers to make the process as seamless as possible for you.

  • Full structural and body damage assessment
  • Computerized frame straightening and measurement
  • OEM panel replacement and fitting
  • Insurance claim support and direct billing
  • Pre- and post-repair documentation
  • Delivery detail and quality inspection

Service 02

Expert Body Shop

Our body shop is staffed by craftsmen who have dedicated their careers to working on the world's most demanding vehicles. The tolerances on a Ferrari door or a Rolls-Royce quarter panel demand a level of skill that can only come from years of focused experience.

We hold the work to the same standard the original manufacturer did — and we don't release a vehicle until we're certain it meets that standard.

  • Panel fabrication and custom metalwork
  • Door, hood, fender, and bumper replacement
  • Glass removal, replacement, and sealing
  • Carbon fiber panel repair and replacement
  • Convertible and soft-top hood repair
  • Plastic, composite, and exotic material repair

Service 03

Mechanical & Electrical Support

A significant collision often affects more than just the bodywork. Hidden mechanical and electrical damage can compromise safety and performance long after a vehicle looks perfect on the outside. Our integrated approach means mechanical and body repairs are handled together — under one roof.

Nothing goes unnoticed when we perform a post-collision teardown inspection.

  • Post-collision mechanical inspection and repair
  • Cooling system and radiator replacement
  • Suspension geometry correction and alignment
  • Airbag and SRS system repair and reset
  • Wiring harness inspection and repair
  • ECU and module recalibration post-repair

Service 04

Minor Refinishing & Dent Removal

Not every blemish requires a full panel replacement. Our technicians are skilled in paintless dent repair and minor refinishing techniques that can restore your vehicle's appearance quickly and cost-effectively — without impacting the original factory finish.

We assess each situation individually to recommend the most appropriate and value-preserving solution.

  • Paintless dent repair (PDR) for dings and hail damage
  • Scratch and scuff removal
  • Spot paint blending and panel refinishing
  • Bumper scuff and crack repair
  • Door edge and keyhole scratch repair
  • Oxidation and paint fade correction

Service 05

Paint Restoration

The paint on an exotic or European vehicle is more than color — it is an extension of the engineering and identity of the machine. Our paint restoration process uses factory-matched formulations, precision spray booths, and multi-stage correction techniques to deliver a finish that is indistinguishable from — and often superior to — the original.

From single-panel repairs to complete resprays, we treat every square inch with equal care.

  • Full vehicle and panel-by-panel resprays
  • Factory color matching using spectrophotometry
  • Multi-stage paint correction and polishing
  • Clear coat application and UV protection
  • Custom color and bespoke finish work
  • Post-paint ceramic coating application

Service 06

Rim Refinishing

Your wheels are the first thing people see — and one of the first things to show wear. Curb rash, brake dust etching, and peeling clear coat can diminish the appearance of even the most stunning vehicle. Our rim refinishing service restores factory finishes and can accommodate custom colors and finishes for a personalized touch.

We handle forged alloy, carbon fiber, and specialty wheels used on exotic platforms.

  • Curb rash and scratch repair
  • Full strip, refinish, and clear coat
  • Custom color and powder coat finishing
  • Forged alloy and carbon fiber wheel service
  • Structural crack inspection and repair assessment
  • TPMS sensor reinstallation and calibration

Service 07

Classic & Antique Restoration

Classic and vintage vehicles deserve the same level of devotion they were built with. Aura Motor Lab approaches classic restoration as a form of preservation — honoring the original design intent while applying modern techniques to ensure structural integrity and lasting finish quality.

Every restoration project is a collaboration between our team and the owner, guided by history and executed with passion.

  • Full body-off restoration for vintage and classic vehicles
  • Period-correct panel fabrication and metalwork
  • Historically accurate paint matching and finishing
  • Interior trim and upholstery referral partnerships
  • Mechanical restoration and engine rebuilds
  • Concours preparation and show-quality detailing
