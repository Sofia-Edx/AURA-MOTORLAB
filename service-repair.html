Service & Repair

From routine maintenance to complex diagnostics — our factory-certified master technicians deliver precision care for your European or exotic vehicle.

Service 01

Vehicle Inspection

A thorough inspection is the foundation of every service we perform. Our technicians conduct a comprehensive multi-point assessment to evaluate the current condition of your vehicle and identify any potential issues before they become costly repairs.

Whether it's a pre-purchase inspection or a routine check, we give you an honest, detailed report on every system.

  • Multi-point safety inspection covering all major systems
  • Pre-purchase inspection for high-value acquisitions
  • Brake system and rotor measurement
  • Tire wear, alignment, and pressure assessment
  • Fluid levels and condition analysis
  • Underbody and chassis inspection

Service 02

Digital Diagnostics

Modern exotic and European vehicles are sophisticated machines with complex electronic architectures. Our state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment connects directly to factory-level systems, reading fault codes and live data streams that general shops simply cannot access.

We use the same tools as the manufacturers — so nothing gets missed.

  • Factory-level OBD and CAN bus diagnostics
  • Engine management system analysis
  • Transmission and gearbox module scanning
  • ABS, traction control, and stability system checks
  • ADAS and driver-assist system calibration
  • Electrical fault tracing and wiring diagnostics

Service 03

Scheduled Maintenance

Keeping your exotic or European vehicle on its manufacturer-recommended service schedule is the single most important thing you can do to preserve performance, reliability, and resale value. Aura Motor Lab performs all scheduled services at a fraction of the dealership cost — without compromising on parts or expertise.

We use OEM and OEM-equivalent parts and fluids specified for your exact vehicle.

  • Oil and filter service with manufacturer-specified fluids
  • Spark plug and ignition service
  • Timing belt and chain inspection or replacement
  • Air and cabin filter service
  • Coolant system flush and refill
  • Transmission and differential fluid service
  • Brake fluid and hydraulic system service

Service 04

Brakes, Steering & Suspension

High-performance vehicles demand high-performance stopping power and handling precision. Our technicians are experienced with the advanced braking systems — including carbon-ceramic setups — and complex suspension geometries found on European and exotic platforms.

We restore the dynamics your car was engineered to deliver.

  • High-performance and carbon-ceramic brake service
  • Brake pad, rotor, and caliper replacement
  • Steering rack and power steering service
  • Coilover, air suspension, and damper replacement
  • Control arm, bushing, and ball joint service
  • Four-wheel alignment with OEM specifications
  • Wheel bearing inspection and replacement

Service 05

Air Conditioning

A properly functioning climate control system is essential in any vehicle — especially a luxury one. We service and repair all types of A/C systems found in European and exotic cars, from conventional refrigerant systems to the advanced dual-zone and heat-pump systems in modern hybrids and EVs.

We ensure your cabin environment is always exactly as you want it.

  • A/C system inspection and leak detection
  • Refrigerant recharge and recovery (R-134a and R-1234yf)
  • Compressor, condenser, and evaporator replacement
  • Cabin air filter and blower motor service
  • Dual-zone and multi-zone climate control diagnosis
  • Heat-pump system service for hybrid and EV models

Service 06

Powertrain Excellence

The heart of any performance vehicle is its powertrain. Our master technicians have hands-on experience with the flat-sixes, V8s, V10s, and V12s that define the world's finest automobiles. From minor oil leaks to complete engine rebuilds, we handle it all with surgical precision.

Every repair is performed to factory tolerances — no shortcuts, no compromises.

  • Engine diagnostics and internal inspection
  • Oil leak diagnosis and repair
  • Turbocharger and supercharger service
  • Clutch and dual-clutch transmission service
  • Automatic gearbox diagnostics and service
  • Driveshaft, CV joint, and differential repair
  • Engine rebuilds and long-block replacement
